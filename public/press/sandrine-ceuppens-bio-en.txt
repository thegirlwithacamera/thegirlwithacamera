LONG BIO

My name is Sandrine Ceuppens. I live and work in Brussels. I had a camera in my hands as a child, then life took over and I put it down. In 2024 I picked it up again. What came back wasn't just a hobby, it was a way of seeing.

I photograph streets, light, quiet moments. The kind that go unnoticed. My work sits at the intersection of documentary and fashion, with minimal post-processing — I barely touch the image. Just what's there, as it was.

Beyond stills, I create video content : scripting, filming, editing. I collaborate with Ricoh and Pentax Europe, and I'm building a body of personal series I'd love to see in print.
